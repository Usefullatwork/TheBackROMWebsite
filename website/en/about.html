About Me

Chiropractor Mads Finstad - Your partner for better health and well-being

Mads Finstad: Chiropractor

My name is Mads Finstad, and I work as a chiropractor at Clinic for All Majorstua in Oslo. I help you with dizziness and pain in back, neck, shoulders, hips, knees � and the entire body.

My approach: First understand why you have pain, then create a treatment plan to get you back to what you love. I use chiropractic, dry needling, shockwave and rehabilitation. For dizziness I have special expertise (see dizziness treatment).

I am educated at the Anglo-European College of Chiropractic (AECC) in England.

From sports injury to chiropractor - my personal journey

My path into chiropractic started on the sports field. During years of American football, handball and underwater rugby, I learned how much injuries can limit what you love. A serious shoulder injury became a turning point. Meeting a skilled chiropractor gave me pain relief and inspiration for my future career.

This experience shapes how I meet my patients today. I know how frustrating it is when the body doesn't work as it should, and I understand the importance of being listened to, thoroughly examined and getting a clear treatment plan.

I first studied radiology, but my experience led me to the AECC Chiropractic program. Now my goal is to give the same help to others that I once received.

My Approach: Evidence-Based and Patient-Centered

I see each patient as a unique puzzle. My approach is both evidence-based and patient-centered � I keep up to date with research, but most importantly I listen to you.

My work always starts with thorough detective work to understand why you have pain, not just where the pain is. By combining science, experience, and your preferences, we create a treatment plan tailored to you.

I focus on active treatments and adjust the plan along the way. The goal is to give you the tools and guidance you need to take an active role in your own health, both now and in the future.
