About hip pain

Hip pain can significantly impact your mobility and quality of life, affecting your ability to walk, climb stairs, sit comfortably, or participate in physical activities. The hip is a large ball-and-socket joint that bears much of the body's weight and is essential for movement and stability. Pain can originate from the hip joint itself, surrounding muscles, ligaments, tendons, or nearby structures such as the lower back or pelvis.

Common causes of hip pain include osteoarthritis, hip impingement syndrome, bursitis, tendinitis, muscle strains, labral tears, and referred pain from the lower back or sacroiliac joint. Risk factors include age, previous injuries, overuse from sports or work activities, poor posture, and biomechanical imbalances. Symptoms may include pain in the hip, groin, buttock, or thigh, stiffness, reduced range of motion, and difficulty with weight-bearing activities.

At TheBackROM, we provide comprehensive hip pain treatment using evidence-based approaches. Our treatment may include gentle mobilization techniques to imprøve joint function, soft tissue therapy to address muscle tension and trigger points, specific exercises to strengthen the hip muscles and imprøve stability, biomechanical assessment to identify contributing factors, and postural correction. We take a holistic approach, often addressing related areas such as the lower back, pelvis, and knee to ensure optimal hip function and prevent future problems.
