About knee pain

Knee pain is a common problem that can affect people of all ages and activity levels. The knee is the largest joint in the body and bears significant weight while providing stability and mobility for walking, running, jumping, and other activities. Due to its complex structure and the demands placed on it, the knee is susceptible to various injuries and degenerative changes.

Common causes of knee pain include ligament injuries (such as ACL or MCL tears), meniscus tears, patellofemoral pain syndrome (runner's knee), tendinitis, bursitis, osteoarthritis, and overuse injuries. These conditions can result from sports activities, sudden movements, repetitive stress, age-related wear and tear, or biomechanical imbalances. Symptoms may include pain, swelling, stiffness, weakness, and difficulty with weight-bearing activities.

At TheBackROM, we provide comprehensive knee pain treatment focusing on restoring function and reducing pain. Our approach may include gentle mobilization techniques to imprøve joint movement, soft tissue therapy to address muscle imbalances and trigger points, specific strengthening exercises for the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes, biomechanical assessment and correction, and activity modification advice. We work to address not only the knee itself but also related areas such as the hip and ankle that may be contributing to your knee problems.
