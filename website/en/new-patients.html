First time seeing a chiropractor?

Everything you need to know before your first visit with us

What can you expect on your first visit?

Your first visit to a chiropractor can be an exciting start on the path to better health. We understand that it can be nerve-wracking to visit a chiropractor for the first time, so we have created this guide to prepare you.

The initial consultation takes 45 minutes and includes:

  • Thorough history - we review your health history
  • Discussion of your current problems and symptoms
  • Physical examination and functional tests
  • Explanation of your findings and diagnosis
  • Development of a personalized treatment plan
  • Initial treatment if appropriate

How to prepare for your visit

Bring with you:

  • Valid identification (passport, driver's license or ID card)
  • Any referral from a doctor
  • Previous X-rays or MRI images if you have them
  • List of medications you use
  • Comfortable clothes that you can move easily in

Before the visit:

  • Think about when your problems started
  • Note what makes your problems better or worse
  • Write down questions you want to ask
  • Arrive 10 minutes before the scheduled time

Examination and diagnosis process

Our thorough examination follows a structured approach to find the cause of your problems:

History taking

  • Detailed review of your current symptoms
  • Previous injuries and surgeries
  • Lifestyle and work conditions
  • Exercise activity and hobbies

Physical examination

  • Observation of posture and movement patterns
  • Mobility tests for joints and muscles
  • Neurological tests when needed
  • Orthopedic special tests
  • Palpation (examination with hands)

Your personalized treatment plan

Based on the examination, we develop a tailored treatment plan that may include:

Treatment methods

  • Chiropractic manipulation
  • Soft tissue techniques
  • Dry needling
  • Exercises and rehabilitation

Follow-up

  • Number of treatments recommended
  • Frequency of treatments
  • Home exercises and self-treatment
  • Lifestyle advice and prevention

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a referral from a doctor?

No, you don't need a referral from a doctor to visit a chiropractor. You can book an appointment directly with us. If you have a referral, please bring it as it can provide useful information.

Does chiropractic treatment hurt?

Chiropractic treatment should not be painful. You may experience a "crack" or "click" during manipulation, but this is normal and painless. We always adapt the treatment to your comfort.

How many treatments do I need?

This varies from person to person and depends on your condition. Acute problems can often be resolved in 2-4 treatments, while chronic conditions may require longer treatment courses. We discuss this thoroughly during the first consultation.

Can I drive after treatment?

Yes, most people can drive after treatment. In rare cases, you may experience mild dizziness or muscle soreness, so we recommend waiting a few minutes after treatment before driving.

What does the initial consultation cost?

The initial consultation costs 1200 Norwegian kroner and includes thorough examination, diagnosis, and initial treatment. See our price page for a complete overview of all our services.
