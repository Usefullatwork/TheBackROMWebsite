Privacy Policy

I am committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in a transparent and secure manner

This privacy policy describes how TheBackROM collects, uses and protects your personal data. Your privacy is important to me, and I am committed to complying with applicable privacy legislation.

1. Data Controller

Klinikk for alle, represented by Mads Finstad, is the data controller for personal data collected through this website.

Contact information:

2. What information do I collect?

I may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, when you fill out the contact form or book an appointment.
  • Communication: Inquiries and other communication you send to me.
  • Technical data: Information about your device, IP address, browser type, and how you navigate the site. This is collected via cookies.
  • Analytics information: Anonymized data about site usage to improve the user experience.

3. Purpose of collection

I use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries via the contact form
  • To manage appointment bookings and treatment plans
  • To improve and optimize website functionality and user experience
  • To send relevant information about our services (only with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal basis

The processing of your personal data is based on:

  • Consent: When you fill out contact forms or subscribe to newsletters
  • Contract performance: For administration of appointment bookings and treatment
  • Legitimate interest: For technical data and analytics to provide a functional and secure website
  • Legal obligation: To comply with healthcare personnel legislation and other relevant laws

5. Sharing of personal data

I do not share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • With data processors who help us with IT services (these are bound by data processing agreements)
  • In anonymized form for statistical purposes

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Improve your user experience
  • Analyze how the website is used
  • Remember your preferences
  • Ensure the website functions optimally

You can manage or delete cookies via your browser settings. Please note that this may affect the functionality of the website.

7. Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request access to the personal data I have stored about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise your rights, contact me at Mads.finstad@hotmail.com

8. Data storage and security

I take data security seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Alteration or deletion
  • Loss or damage
  • Accidental processing

Personal data is only retained for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

9. Right to complain

If you believe our processing of personal data is in breach of the GDPR, you have the right to complain to the Norwegian Data Protection Authority:

0105 Oslo

10. Changes to the privacy policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or relevant legislation. The latest version will always be available on our website.
