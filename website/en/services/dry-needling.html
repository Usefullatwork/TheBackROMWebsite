Dry Needling

Advanced technique for treating trigger points and muscle tension

What is dry needling?

Dry needling is a modern treatment technique that uses thin needles to treat muscle tension, trigger points, and pain conditions. Unlike acupuncture, dry needling is based on Western medical principles and targets specific anatomical structures such as trigger points, tight muscle bands, and connective tissue.

At TheBackROM, we use dry needling as part of our comprehensive treatment approach to help relieve muscle tension, reduce pain, and imprøve function. The technique can be particularly effective for chronic muscle pain, sports injuries, and conditions that haven't responded well to other treatments.
