INSTAGRAM THUMBNAIL IMAGES

This folder is for storing thumbnail images of your Instagram posts that will be displayed on the website.

Instructions:
1. Take screenshots or download thumbnails of your Instagram posts
2. Rename them to post1.jpg, post2.jpg, post3.jpg, etc.
3. Place them in this folder
4. Make sure the images are square and high quality

Recommended image specifications:
- Square aspect ratio (1:1)
- Minimum size: 300x300 pixels
- Recommended size: 600x600 pixels
- File format: JPG or PNG
- Optimized for web (compressed but still good quality)

Note: The images should correspond to the Instagram post URLs in the index.html file.
