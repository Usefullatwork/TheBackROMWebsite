Privacy Policy for TheBackROM

This privacy policy explains how TheBackROM collects, uses and protects your personal data. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Data Controller

Klinikk for alle, represented by Mads Finstad, is the data controller for the personal data collected through this website.

2. What information do we collect?

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number when you submit our contact form.
  • Communication: Messages or inquiries you send to us.
  • Technical data: Information about your device, IP address, browser type, and how you navigate the site. This is collected via cookies.

3. Purpose of processing

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries via the contact form.
  • To improve and optimize the website's functionality and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal basis

The processing of your data is based on your consent when you submit the contact form. Technical data are processed based on our legitimate interest in providing å functional and secure website.

5. Sharing of personal data

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. These are small text files stored on your device. You can manage or delete cookies via your browser settings.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Lodge å complaint with your data protection authority if you believe our processing violates applicable rules.

8. Contact

If you have questions about our processing of personal data, contact us at:

Email: Mads.finstad@hotmail.com

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.
